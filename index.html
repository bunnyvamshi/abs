Eligibility Criteria

  • A valid GMAT or GRE score
  • A bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Minimum 24 months of full-time work experience after graduation.

Note: Candidates with less than 24 months of work experience can still apply to ASB through two other modes of entry.

Young Leaders Programme (YLP) - This route is for high-calibre final and pre-final year college students.

Early Entry Option (EEO) - Candidates with less than 2 years of work experience can apply via this route.

Regarded among the best B-Schools in the world, ISB endows young managers with multidisciplinary perspectives and global exposure. The programme is designed to enhance business management skills with cutting-edge research and a widely acclaimed curriculum taught by the best faculty in the world.

    ASB’S GLOBAL IMMERSION PROGRAM

    Understanding International Business and Management practise are quintessential in today’s Globalized Business Environment. Our students go through a one-week immersion program in Singapore. The trip includes several lectures by world-renowned faculty, multiple industry visits, workshops on Singaporean Culture and Economy etc.

    EXECUTIVES IN RESIDENCE PROGRAM (EIR)

    The EIR program intends to bring Senior Executives, from various Businesses including Technology, FMCG, Health Care, Finance and Investments, Retail and Consulting, together to form a group of Executive in Residence who will address students through seminars, lectures and Q&A sessions and take up the role of mentoring students to make them industry-ready by the time they graduate. There will be two sessions of 4 hours a month for this program.
